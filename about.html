Who We Are

Collections by BK Proctor is a premium fashion brand based in Botswana, specializing in high-quality footwear, accessories, and apparel. Founded in 2018, we've grown from a small local business to one of Botswana's most recognized fashion brands.

Our mission is to combine contemporary style with traditional craftsmanship, creating products that stand out for their quality, comfort, and unique design.

BK Proctor

Founder & Creative Director

"I started this brand with a vision to put Botswana on the global fashion map. Every piece we create tells a story of our heritage and our aspirations."
